Our Story

Building a legacy of faith and excellence

Since 2025, Franciscan Catholic School has been nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders through a holistic education rooted in Catholic values. Founded by the Franciscan Sisters of the Immaculate Conception, our school began with a vision to provide quality education that develops not only academic excellence but also spiritual growth and character formation.

Over the years, we have grown from a small parish school to a respected educational institution, while maintaining our commitment to personalized attention and a supportive learning environment. Our dedicated staff, many of whom have been with us for over a decade, create a family atmosphere where every child feels valued and encouraged to reach their full potential.

Today, Franciscan Catholic School continues to build on its rich tradition while embracing innovative teaching methods to prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century. Our graduates consistently demonstrate not only academic achievement but also strong moral character and a commitment to service.

Our Ethos

The principles that guide our educational approach

Our educational ethos is rooted in the Franciscan tradition, emphasizing simplicity, respect for all creation, and joyful service. We believe in developing the whole child through:

Faith Formation

We integrate Catholic teachings throughout our curriculum and daily activities, helping students develop a personal relationship with God and understand how faith guides ethical decision-making and service to others.

Academic Excellence

We maintain high academic standards through a rigorous curriculum that emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity. Our teaching methods are designed to engage different learning styles and foster a love for lifelong learning.

Character Development

We emphasize the development of virtues such as honesty, responsibility, respect, and compassion. Through both structured character education and everyday interactions, we help students build strong moral foundations.

Global Citizenship

We prepare students to be responsible global citizens who understand diverse cultures, care for the environment, and contribute positively to society. Through service projects and community engagement, students learn to put their faith into action.
