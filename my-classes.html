Lesson Plan

Primary 5 - Mathematics

May 15, 2023

Topic

Fractions and Decimals

Learning Objectives

  • Convert fractions to decimals and vice versa
  • Compare and order fractions and decimals
  • Solve problems involving fractions and decimals

Resources

  • Textbook: Mathematics for Primary 5, Chapter 4
  • Fraction charts and decimal place value charts
  • Worksheets for practice
  • Interactive whiteboard activities

Lesson Structure

10 mins
Introduction

Review previous knowledge on fractions. Introduce the concept of decimals as another way to represent fractions.

15 mins
Teacher Demonstration

Demonstrate how to convert fractions to decimals using division. Show examples with different types of fractions.

15 mins
Guided Practice

Students work in pairs to convert given fractions to decimals. Teacher circulates to provide support.

10 mins
Independent Practice

Students complete worksheet with fraction to decimal conversion problems.

5 mins
Conclusion

Review key concepts. Preview next lesson on comparing fractions and decimals.

Assessment

Formative assessment through observation during guided practice. Worksheet completion will be checked for understanding.

Homework

Complete textbook exercises on page 45, questions 1-10.

Notes

Pay special attention to students who struggled with fractions in previous lessons. Provide additional support as needed.
