Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What are the school hours?

Our school hours are from 7:30 AM to 2:30 PM, Monday through Friday. We offer after-school programs until 5:00 PM for parents who need extended care.

What is the admission process?

The admission process involves completing an application form, submitting required documents, an assessment test for the child, and an interview with parents. Please contact our admissions office for detailed information.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we offer transportation services to select areas around Lagos. The service is optional and comes with an additional fee. Our buses are equipped with safety features and are managed by experienced drivers and attendants.

What curriculum do you follow?

We follow a blended curriculum that integrates the Nigerian National Curriculum with international best practices. Our approach emphasizes Catholic values, academic excellence, and holistic development.

Are meals provided at school?

We provide nutritious meals prepared in our school kitchen. Parents can opt for the meal plan or send packed lunches. Our menu is carefully designed to meet the nutritional needs of growing children.
