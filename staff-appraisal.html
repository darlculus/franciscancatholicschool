Self-Appraisal Form

Section 1: Teaching Effectiveness

Evaluate your teaching methods, student engagement, and classroom management.

Rate your effectiveness in preparing comprehensive lesson plans that align with curriculum standards.

Rate your ability to maintain discipline and create a positive learning environment.

Rate your effectiveness in engaging students and promoting active participation.

Section 2: Professional Development

Evaluate your growth as an educator and participation in professional development activities.

Rate your commitment to improving your teaching skills through workshops, courses, or other learning opportunities.

Rate your effectiveness in applying new teaching methods or technologies in your classroom.

Section 3: Collaboration and School Community

Evaluate your contributions to the school community and collaboration with colleagues.

Rate your effectiveness in working with colleagues and contributing to team efforts.

Rate your effectiveness in communicating with parents and involving them in their child's education.

Section 4: Goals and Development Plan

Reflect on your achievements and set goals for the upcoming academic year.

List your major accomplishments from the previous academic year.

Describe any significant challenges you encountered and how you addressed them.

Outline your professional development goals for the upcoming academic year.

Describe any support, resources, or training you need to achieve your goals.

Appraisal Guidelines

Purpose of Staff Appraisal

The staff appraisal process at Franciscan Catholic Nursery and Primary School is designed to:

  • Recognize and celebrate teaching excellence
  • Identify areas for professional growth and development
  • Align individual goals with school objectives
  • Provide constructive feedback for continuous improvement
  • Support career development and progression

Appraisal Process Timeline

1

Self-Appraisal

Complete your self-assessment form by September 30, 2025

2

Supervisor Review

Your supervisor will review your self-appraisal and provide their assessment by October 15, 2025

3

Appraisal Discussion

Meet with your supervisor to discuss the appraisal between October 20-30, 2025

4

Final Submission

Review and sign the final appraisal document by November 5, 2025

Rating Scale Explanation

1

Needs Improvement

Performance does not meet expected standards. Significant improvement is required in this area.

2

Fair

Performance occasionally meets expected standards but is inconsistent. Development is needed in this area.

3

Satisfactory

Performance consistently meets expected standards. This represents solid, effective performance.

4

Good

Performance frequently exceeds expected standards. This represents strong, consistent performance.

5

Excellent

Performance consistently exceeds expected standards by a significant margin. This represents exceptional performance.

Tips for Effective Self-Appraisal

  • Be honest and objective in your self-assessment
  • Provide specific examples to support your ratings
  • Focus on both strengths and areas for improvement
  • Set SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
  • Reflect on your professional growth over the past academic year
  • Consider feedback from students, parents, and colleagues

Confidentiality

All information shared during the appraisal process is confidential and will only be accessible to you, your supervisor, and authorized administrative personnel. The information will be used solely for professional development purposes and will not be shared with unauthorized individuals.

For any questions regarding the appraisal process, please contact the Human Resources Department.

Sample Appraisal Form

This is a sample form for reference only. The actual form may vary slightly when the appraisal cycle begins.

Teaching Effectiveness

Lesson Planning and Preparation

Sample Comment: "I develop detailed lesson plans that align with curriculum standards and include differentiated activities to meet diverse learning needs. I could improve by incorporating more technology-based resources."

Professional Development

Continuous Learning

Sample Comment: "I attended three professional development workshops this year, including 'Innovative Teaching Methods' and 'Technology in the Classroom.' I also completed an online course on inclusive education and shared the resources with colleagues."

Goals and Development Plan

Professional Goals

Sample Response: "1. Implement project-based learning in at least one unit per term. 2. Develop expertise in educational technology by completing two relevant courses. 3. Mentor a new teacher in our department."
