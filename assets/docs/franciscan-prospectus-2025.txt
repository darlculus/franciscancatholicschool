✨ OUR CORE VALUES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
FAITH
Deepening our relationship with God through daily prayer, sacraments, 
and Franciscan spirituality, guiding students to live as Christ's disciples.

STEWARDSHIP
Cultivating excellence in academics and character by nurturing God-given 
talents for service to others and care for creation.

SOLIDARITY
Building a compassionate community where students, families, and staff 
collaborate to uplift the marginalized and foster mutual dignity.

INTEGRITY
Developing moral character through honesty, responsibility, and 
ethical decision-making in all aspects of life.

📋 ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Required Documents:
• Completed application form
• Birth certificate (original and photocopy)
• Recent passport photographs (4 copies)
• Immunization record
• Previous school report (for transfer students)
• Medical report from a recognized hospital
• Parent/Guardian identification

Admission Process:
1. Submit completed application form
2. Schedule entrance assessment (for Primary applicants)
3. Parent-child interview
4. Admission decision notification
5. Fee payment and registration
