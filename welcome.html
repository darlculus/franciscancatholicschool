Message from the Principal

Guiding our school with vision and dedication

Rev. Sr. Victoria Ogunwade

School Principal

Dear Parents and Guardians,

Welcome to Franciscan Catholic Nursery & Primary School, where every child's potential is nurtured and celebrated. Our commitment to excellence in education goes beyond academic achievement â€“ we focus on developing well-rounded individuals equipped with the skills and values needed for success in today's world.

At Franciscan Catholic Nursery & Primary School, we believe that every child is unique and deserves an education that recognizes and develops their individual talents. Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to create an environment where learning is engaging, meaningful, and fun.

Our Catholic foundation guides our approach to education, instilling values of compassion, integrity, and service in our students. We strive to create a nurturing community where children can grow spiritually, intellectually, and socially.

The partnership between home and school is vital to a child's success. We encourage parents to be actively involved in their child's education and to maintain open communication with our staff. Together, we can provide the support and guidance that will help your child thrive.

We invite you to join our community where we work together to build a strong foundation for your child's future.

In Christ's service,

Rev. Sr. Victoria Ogunwade
School Principal

Rev. Sr. Victoria Ogunwade

School Principal, Franciscan Catholic School

Rev. Sr. Victoria Ogunwade is a dedicated educator and member of the Franciscan Sisters of the Immaculate Conception. With over 20 years of experience in Catholic education, she brings spiritual guidance and educational expertise to our school community.

Sister Victoria's vision for Franciscan Catholic School is rooted in the Catholic tradition of education that nurtures the whole child - spiritually, intellectually, emotionally, and physically. Under her leadership, our school has flourished as a center of academic excellence and faith formation.

Her dedication to continuous improvement and innovation in education ensures that our students receive a modern education grounded in timeless values.

Educational Background

  • M.Ed in Educational Leadership - University of Ibadan
  • B.Ed in Religious Education - University of Lagos
  • Diploma in Catholic School Administration - Rome

Professional Experience

  • 20+ years in Catholic Education
  • Former Vice Principal at St. Mary's College
  • Member, Association of Catholic School Administrators
  • Coordinator, Diocesan Education Committee
