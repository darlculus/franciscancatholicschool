Nurturing Excellence in Faith and Learning

At Franciscan Catholic Nursery and Primary School, we believe in providing a nurturing environment where children can grow intellectually, spiritually, and socially. Founded on the principles of Catholic education and the Franciscan tradition, our school offers a balanced curriculum that prepares learners for academic success while instilling strong moral values.

Our dedicated staff are committed to recognizing each child's unique gifts and talents, fostering a love for learning that extends beyond the classroom. We invite you to explore what makes our school community unique.

Our Core Values

The pillars that guide our educational approach

Faith

Deepening our relationship with God through daily prayer, sacraments, and Franciscan spirituality, guiding learners to live as Christ's disciples.

Dedication

Excel academically and morally by honing God-given talents, serving purposefully, and sustaining creation through perseverance.

Compassion

Building a compassionate community where learners, families, and staff collaborate to uplift the marginalized and foster mutual dignity.

Integrity

Developing moral character through honesty, responsibility, and ethical decision-making in all aspects of life.
