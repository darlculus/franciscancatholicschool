Franciscan Catholic Nursery and Primary School

"Quality Catholic Education Rooted in Franciscan Values"

Welcome to Our School Family

At Franciscan Catholic Nursery and Primary School, we believe in providing a nurturing environment where children can grow intellectually, spiritually, and socially. Founded on the principles of Catholic education and the Franciscan tradition, our school offers a balanced curriculum that prepares students for academic success while instilling strong moral values.

Our dedicated teachers and staff are committed to recognizing each child's unique gifts and talents, fostering a love for learning that extends beyond the classroom.

Our Core Values

Faith

Deepening our relationship with God through daily prayer, sacraments, and Franciscan spirituality.

Stewardship

Cultivating excellence in academics and character by nurturing God-given talents.

Solidarity

Building a compassionate community where everyone collaborates to foster mutual dignity.

Integrity

Developing moral character through honesty, responsibility, and ethical decision-making.

Academic Programmes

Early Years Foundation (Ages 1-4)

  • Creche (Ages 1-2): Basic care, sensory development, and early socialization
  • Pre-Nursery (Age 3): Introduction to structured learning through play
  • Nursery (Age 4): Pre-literacy and numeracy skills, creative arts

Primary Education (Ages 5-11)

  • Lower Primary (Ages 5-8): Core subjects with emphasis on literacy and numeracy
  • Upper Primary (Ages 9-11): Advanced curriculum preparing for secondary education

Core Subjects

Admission Requirements

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original and photocopy)
  • Recent passport photographs (4 copies)
  • Immunization record
  • Previous school report (for transfer students)
  • Medical report from a recognized hospital
  • Parent/Guardian identification

Admission Process

  1. Submit completed application form
  2. Schedule entrance assessment (for Primary applicants)
  3. Parent-child interview
  4. Admission decision notification
  5. Fee payment and registration
